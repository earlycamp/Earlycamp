Welcome to Earlycamp where we are driven by a singular purpose: to put quality and practical technical education within the reach of every child. We do this through inspiring, training and mentoring children in technology...

Our Commitment

Earlycamp is a vibrant and fun place, with focused caring guides and a committed parent body. Every child joining Earlycamp will find that they can develop technically, artistically and socially. They learn to be confident and to fulfil their aspirations.
Our areas of focus are:
  • Computer science.
  • Programming.
  • Presentation skills.
  • Social skills.
  • The Internet of things.
  • Robotics.

    So far

    We started in a living room, inspired by our mission to put quality technical education within the reach of every child. Today our children have accumulated over 54 hours of coding practice. We have held over 25 sessions with children and held 2 holiday boot camps. For the last couple of months, we have been the trusted choice for families in Nairobi and beyond to mentor, train and inspire their children in technology.
